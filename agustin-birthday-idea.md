# Agustin's 9th Birthday Invite - Idea Plan

## Theme
**"Lego Mystery Adventure"** - A mystery adventure combining LEGO building elements with Scooby Doo themes.

## Core Direction
- Keep the playful mystery vibe, but make every screen very fast to understand
- Prioritize tap-first interactions over drag-and-drop
- Keep text short and visual so it works well on mobile

## Structure (3 Levels)

### Level 1 - The Clue Builder
**Mechanic**: Tap or drag LEGO-style brick elements to reveal hidden clues

**How it works**:
- Screen shows a "mystery board" with LEGO-style background
- 3 LEGO bricks are placed on top (colorful, big tap targets)
- Kids tap each brick to reveal what's underneath, or drag if they want
- Each brick reveals a clue/number needed for Level 2

**Theme elements**:
- Scooby Doo mystery board aesthetic
- LEGO brick styling
- Colors: Red, Yellow, Blue, Green (LEGO primary colors)
- Subtle Scooby Doo references (Scooby snacks, medallion, etc.)

---

### Level 2 - The Logic Puzzle
**Mechanic**: Kid-friendly visual puzzle - "Find the right clue"

**How it works**:
- Show 3 or 4 character or door options with big, clear images
- Use one short clue to help kids pick the right answer
- Wrong answers show a "Scooby Snack" penalty (fun, not frustrating)
- Correct answer reveals the party location

**The Puzzle** (example):
- "Only one door has the party clue. Pick the right one!"
- Keep it visual first, with very little reading

**Theme elements**:
- LEGO-style character figures
- Mystery machine reference
- Scooby Doo color palette

---

### Level 3 - The Reveal
**Mechanic**: Final celebration reveal

**How it works**:
- Confetti animation (LEGO bricks falling or Scooby Doo themed)
- Birthday invitation card appears with:
  - Date
  - Time
  - Location
  - Age (turning 9!)
- Add a clear "Play again" button if they want to retry

**Theme elements**:
- LEGO balloons
- Scooby Snacks as decoration
- Party celebration vibe
- "Happy Birthday Agustin" message

---

## Visual Style

**Colors**:
- Primary: LEGO Red (#E3000B), LEGO Yellow (#FFD500), LEGO Blue (#0055BF), LEGO Green (#009B48)
- Background: Dark mystery vibe (deep blue/purple) to match both themes
- Accents: Orange (Scooby), Purple (mystery)
- UI: Large contrasty buttons and readable text on small screens

**Images needed**:
- Level 1: LEGO mystery board background + brick images
- Level 2: LEGO-style character images + door images
- Level 3: Party background + invite card design

---

## Mobile-First
- Same approach as the main invite
- Tap-first interactions with large hit areas
- Optimized image sizes
- Minimal text per screen so it loads and reads fast
